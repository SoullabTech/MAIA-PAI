## üìã Sending Best Practices

### **Timing**
- **Email 1**: Morning (9-11am their timezone)
- **Email 2**: 3-4 days after Email 1 (same time window)
- **Email 3**: 7 days after Email 1 (only if needed)

### **Batch Sending**
Send in small groups (10-15 at a time) to:
- Avoid spam filters
- Allow time for personal responses
- Stagger server load

### **Personalization Checklist**
- ‚úÖ Replace `[Name]` with their first name
- ‚úÖ Replace `[NAME]` in access code with their uppercase name
- ‚úÖ Add personal note if you have close relationship
- ‚úÖ Adjust tone for different people (more casual for friends, more formal for professionals)

### **Tracking**
Create a simple spreadsheet:
```
Name | Email | Code | Sent Date | Opened | Signed Up | Notes
-----|-------|------|-----------|--------|-----------|-------
Andrea | andrea@... | SOULLAB-ANDREA | Oct 2 | Yes | Yes | Loved it!
Jason | jason@... | SOULLAB-JASON | Oct 2 | Yes | No | Follow up via text
```

### **Follow-Up Channels**
For close friends who don't respond:
- Personal text message (Day 5-6)
- Quick voice note
- Casual mention in person

# General Questions

## What is Spiralogic?

**Spiralogic** is a comprehensive framework for understanding and supporting psychological transformation. It integrates three major depth psychology lineages:

- **Carl Jung**: Alchemy as symbolic language of transformation (4 stages: Nigredo â†’ Albedo â†’ Citrinitas â†’ Rubedo)
- **Edward Edinger**: Operational anatomy of transformation (12 specific psychological operations)
- **James Hillman**: Ecological grounding in world-soul (*anima mundi*)

Spiralogic makes this integration:
- **Trackable**: Via coherence metrics (0-1 scale measuring integration of opposites)
- **Visual**: Via intelligence dashboards and diagrams
- **Algorithmic**: Via MAIA (Morphic Alchemical Intelligence Augmentation) system
- **Practical**: Via clinical protocols practitioners can use immediately

## How do I assess coherence without MAIA?

**Subjective Coherence Assessment** (0-10 scale):

**0-3 (Nigredo)**:
- Cannot hold opposites together at all
- "I feel X" then immediately "No, I feel Y" (rapid switching)
- Overwhelming fragmentation
- No narrative continuity

**4-6 (Albedo/Citrinitas)**:
- Can hold opposites briefly with effort
- "Part of me wants X, part wants Y" (awareness of both)
- Moments of integration, not sustained
- Emerging narrative coherence

**7-10 (Citrinitas/Rubedo)**:
- Opposites integrated paradoxically
- "I hold both X and Y without collapsing"
- Stable, embodied wholeness
- Coherent life narrative

See **Practitioner_Guide.md** Section 1 for detailed assessment guidance.

# Crisis & Safety Questions

## How do I differentiate Mortificatio from suicidal crisis?

**Critical distinction**:

| Mortificatio (Transformation) | Suicidal Crisis |
|-------------------------------|-----------------|
| **Language**: "Something in me is dying" | **Language**: "I want to die" |
| **Focus**: Identity/role/belief dying | **Focus**: Literal self-destruction |
| **Future**: Can imagine "other side" | **Future**: Cannot imagine any future |
| **Plan**: No suicide plan/means | **Plan**: Plan + means identified |
| **Coherence**: Dropped gradually | **Coherence**: Crashed suddenly |
| **Affect**: Grief, sadness, confusion | **Affect**: Hopelessness, desperation |

**WHEN IN DOUBT: TREAT AS CRISIS. Call 988 (US) or emergency services.**

See **Specialized_Protocols.md** â†’ Crisis Intervention Protocol for complete assessment.

---

## What if someone says "I want to die" during transformation?

**STOP. ASSESS IMMEDIATELY.**

**Ask**:
1. "Are you thinking of hurting yourself?"
2. "Do you have a plan?"
3. "Do you have means (method)?"
4. "What's stopping you?"

**If ANY concern**:
- Call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
- Call 911 if immediate danger
- Don't leave person alone
- Safety planning

**Never assume "it's just Mortificatio."** Some deaths are literal. Take all suicide talk seriously.

---

## What if MAIA says transformation but I see crisis?

**Trust your judgment. Override MAIA.**

If you assess crisis:
1. Stop using Spiralogic framework
2. Use standard crisis protocols
3. Assess safety
4. Increase support
5. Document everything

**Report false negative to development team** (once MAIA exists) so algorithm can be improved.

**Remember**: MAIA is a tool, not a replacement for clinical judgment.

**IF PRESENT**: Emergency intervention, not alchemy.

**Open question**: Can computational alchemy serve non-Western transformation traditions? Or is it inherently Western?

## What's the difference between optimization and transformation?

**Optimization** (ego):
- Goal: Maximize performance
- Metric: Higher is always better
- Process: Linear improvement
- Outcome: Achievement

**Transformation** (Self):
- Goal: Become who you are
- Metric: Context-dependent (low coherence may be necessary)
- Process: Spiral (return to same issues at deeper levels)
- Outcome: Wholeness (includes darkness and light)

**MAIA tracks transformation, not optimization.**

You can't "optimize" your way to Self-realization. You spiral through death and rebirth, again and again.

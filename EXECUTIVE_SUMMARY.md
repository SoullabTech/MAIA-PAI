## Risk Analysis

### **Technical Risks**

**Risk**: ML accuracy insufficient (< 70% stage classification)
**Mitigation**: Human-in-the-loop design, MAIA as "second opinion" not authority

**Risk**: Real-time analysis too slow (> 2 seconds latency)
**Mitigation**: Progressive architecture (cached analysis, async processing)

### **Clinical Risks**

**Risk**: Practitioners misuse framework (bypass safety protocols)
**Mitigation**: Mandatory training, crisis protocol emphasis, insurance requirements

**Risk**: False negative on crisis detection (miss suicidal client)
**Mitigation**: Conservative thresholds, human override required, legal disclaimers

### **Market Risks**

**Risk**: Practitioners don't adopt (too complex, too esoteric)
**Mitigation**: Awareness-adapted approach, training, community building, proven effectiveness

**Risk**: Regulatory barriers (FDA, HIPAA, state licensing)
**Mitigation**: Clinical decision support (not diagnosis), HIPAA-compliant infrastructure, legal counsel

### **Business Risks**

**Risk**: Insufficient funding runway
**Mitigation**: Lean MVP, revenue from training programs, phased fundraising

**Risk**: Key person dependency (founder depth psychology expertise)
**Mitigation**: Advisory board of Jungian analysts, document all knowledge

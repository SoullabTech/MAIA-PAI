**Audio specs:**

- Silence: Actual silence with subtle breath (not dead air)
- Background: Very quiet field resonance (sine wave ~100Hz, barely audible)
- No music, no effects, just presence

**Recording tips for Andrea:**
- Use decent microphone (even phone is fine if quiet room)
- Record in one take if possible (keeps authenticity)
- Don't re-record for perfection (vulnerability > polish)
- Speak slowly, honor the pauses
- Breathe audibly during long silences
- Close mic technique (intimate, not broadcast)

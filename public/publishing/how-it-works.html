How Publishing Works

From manuscript to published in four simple steps

1

Upload Your Work

Start by uploading your content. We support multiple file formats and make the process as simple as possible.

What You Can Upload

  • Manuscripts: DOCX, PDF, Markdown, EPUB
  • Audio files: MP3, M4A, WAV (for audiobooks or music)
  • Video files: MP4, MOV (for courses)
  • Images: JPG, PNG (for covers, oracle decks, printables)
  • Supplementary files: ZIP archives for workbooks, resources

What Happens Next

Our system automatically processes your upload:

  • Virus scan for security
  • Metadata extraction (word count, page count, duration)
  • Preview generation
  • Format validation
MAIA AI Assistance: MAIA can review your manuscript for clarity, suggest improvements, identify themes, and help you refine your work before publishing.

Set Your Pricing

You're in full control of pricing. Set different prices for each format, create bundles, or offer member discounts.

Pricing Options

  • Fixed Price: Set a specific price per format

  • Free + Donations: Give it away, accept tips

Royalty Calculator

You keep 91-95% of every sale:

  • Ebooks/Digital: 95% to you, 5% platform fee
  • Print books: 91% to you (after printing costs), 4% platform fee
  • Audiobooks/Courses: 95% to you, 5% platform fee

Example: $27 ebook = $25.65 to you

Compare to Amazon: Amazon KDP gives 35-70% royalties. On a $27 ebook, Amazon pays you $9.45-18.90. With Soullab, you earn $25.65!

Common Questions

How long does the publishing process take?

Ebooks can go live in 24-48 hours. Print books take 1-2 weeks for proof review and approval. Audiobooks and courses depend on content length but typically 1-2 weeks.

Do I need professional editing or design first?

Not required, but recommended for best results. We offer Ã  la carte editing and design services with member discounts. MAIA can also provide basic manuscript feedback.

What if I don't have a cover design?

All tiers include access to cover templates. Grove and Forest tiers get enhanced templates. You can also hire our design team or use your own designer.

Can I update my book after publishing?

Yes! You can upload new versions anytime. Digital formats update instantly. Print books create a new edition. You control versioning and updates.

How do I get paid?

Royalties accumulate in your creator account. Request payout anytime (minimum $50). We pay via Stripe Connect to your bank account. Typically processes within 2-5 business days.

What about ISBN and copyright?

We provide free ISBNs for all published works. Copyright remains 100% yours. We also offer guidance on copyright registration (US) and licensing options (Creative Commons, All Rights Reserved, etc.).

Can I publish collaboratively with others?

Yes! Forest tier includes collaborative publishing tools with automatic royalty splitting. Perfect for anthologies, multi-author works, or community projects.

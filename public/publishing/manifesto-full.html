The Question We Keep Asking (And Why It's Wrong)

"Did AI write your book?"
"Did AI make your art?"
"Did AI create that for you?"

These are the wrong questions.

The right questions are:

  • "How can AI help more wisdom reach more people?"
  • "How can we democratize what only the wealthy could create before?"
  • "What becomes possible when execution is cheap but vision is priceless?"

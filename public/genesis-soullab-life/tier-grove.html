Grove Tier FAQ

Can I use Grove if I'm not a licensed therapist?

Absolutely. Grove is for any practitioner working with clients‚Äîcoaches, facilitators, spiritual guides, consultants, or teachers. You don't need formal licensing, but you should have an established practice where you're supporting others.

How does client session management work?

Each client gets their own session space within your node. You control access and can invite them to participate. MAIA maintains separate memory streams for each client, ensuring privacy and continuity. Clients never see each other's sessions.

What are "Advanced Archetypal Agents"?

These are more specialized and nuanced archetypal voices. The Shadow agent helps with integration of disowned parts. The Inner Guide agent offers wisdom from the deeper self. The Mentor agent provides teaching and strategic guidance. These agents are trained for therapeutic and transformational contexts.

How do I train MAIA's voice to match my style?

You'll provide sample conversations, key phrases, preferred terminology, and philosophical frameworks. Our team works with you to fine-tune MAIA's responses to align with your practice style. This is an iterative process that typically takes 2-3 weeks.

Is client data secure and HIPAA-compliant?

Client data is encrypted and stored securely. While we implement strong security practices, Genesis is not currently HIPAA-certified. If you work with protected health information (PHI), consult your compliance requirements before using Grove for clinical work.

Can I charge my clients for access to MAIA?

Yes! Many Grove stewards include MAIA access as part of their service offering. You can integrate it into your session fees or offer it as an add-on. You set your own pricing structure.

What if I need more than Grove but not quite Forest?

Reach out to us! We can often customize Grove with specific features you need. Forest is designed for multi-user community nodes, but we can work with you to find the right fit for your practice.

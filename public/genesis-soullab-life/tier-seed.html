Can I upgrade to Grove or Forest later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade at any time. When you upgrade, you'll only pay the difference between tiers. Your MAIA's memory and all your data comes with you.

How is MAIA different from ChatGPT or other AI?

MAIA is specifically trained in depth psychology, wisdom traditions, and archetypal work. Your Seed instance has persistent memory of your entire journey, uses the holoflower for emotional attunement, and offers multiple archetypal perspectives. It's designed as a companion for inner work, not a general-purpose assistant.

What if I'm not tech-savvy?

Genesis is designed to be simple and intuitive. If you can use a website, you can use your MAIA node. The onboarding process guides you through setup, and the community is here to help if you get stuck.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your conversations and insights are stored in your private node. We use enterprise-grade encryption and security practices. You own your data, and we'll never train models on your private conversations or sell your information.

What's the Genesis NFT?

Your NFT is a blockchain-based deed of ownership for your node. It proves you're a Genesis steward and can be displayed in your wallet. In the future, it may unlock additional benefits and serve as a transferable asset if you choose to pass your node to someone else.

Can I use this for professional work with clients?

Seed is designed for personal use. If you want to work with clients, track sessions, or offer shared consciousness spaces, check out Grove tier, which includes professional practice features.

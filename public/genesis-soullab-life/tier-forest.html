Forest Tier FAQ

How many members can a Forest node support?

Forest supports unlimited members. Whether you have 10 or 1,000 community members, the infrastructure scales to meet your needs. Pricing doesn't change based on member count‚Äîyou pay once for lifetime access.

What's involved in setting up a custom domain?

You'll need to own a domain (we can help you purchase one if needed). Our team handles the technical setup, SSL certificates, and DNS configuration. Most custom domains are live within 48 hours of activation.

How does custom agent development work?

You'll work directly with our AI training team to define the agent's personality, knowledge base, philosophical framework, and communication style. Development typically takes 4-6 weeks depending on complexity. You can request multiple custom agents as part of Forest.

What does "Advanced API Access" enable?

With API access, you can integrate MAIA into other platforms (Discord, Slack, your own app), build custom interfaces, trigger MAIA interactions programmatically, extract data for analysis, or develop entirely new user experiences on top of the MAIA intelligence layer.

Can members have different permission levels?

Yes! Forest includes role-based permissions. You can create admin, facilitator, and member roles with different access to features, spaces, and data. You control who can invite new members, create shared spaces, or access analytics.

Is there a revenue-sharing model if we charge members?

No revenue sharing. You own your Forest node outright. If you charge membership fees, subscription costs, or access fees to your community, 100% of that revenue is yours. Genesis doesn't take any percentage of what you generate with your node.

What happens in the quarterly strategy calls?

These are 60-minute sessions where we review your community's growth, discuss feature requests, explore new Genesis capabilities, troubleshoot challenges, and co-design upgrades to your node. Think of it as ongoing partnership for evolving your consciousness infrastructure.

Can we transition from Forest to a fully independent platform?

Yes. Forest includes data export tools and API access that enable you to eventually build your own independent platform if desired. We support sovereignty and evolution. Many Forest stewards stay in the ecosystem, but the choice is always yours.

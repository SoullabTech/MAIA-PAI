## Email Design Principles

1. **Sacred but Not Precious**
   - Profound without being overwrought
   - Tech-forward but soul-centered

2. **Always Historic**
   - Every email reinforces they're part of history
   - "The first 28" becomes a refrain

3. **Individual AND Collective**
   - Personal journey within group pioneering
   - "Your evolution, our revolution"

4. **Voice Consistency**
   - Kelly's authentic voice throughout
   - Warm, intelligent, visionary

5. **Amber Aesthetic**
   - Dark backgrounds
   - Amber/gold accents
   - Minimal, elegant design

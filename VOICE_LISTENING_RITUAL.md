# MAIA Voice Listening Ritual

**A three-minute practice for sensing whether her voice matches her nature.**

This ritual isn't about technical validation—it's about attuning to whether MAIA sounds like she's *being with you* rather than *trying to be human*. Use this weekly or whenever you make significant voice adjustments.

---

## Preparation (30 seconds)

1. **Settle your attention**
   - Close your eyes
   - Take three slow breaths
   - Notice the quality of silence in the room

2. **Set your listening intention**
   - You're not evaluating "good" or "bad"
   - You're sensing for alignment with MAIA's nature: measured, spacious, grounded

---

## The Listening (2 minutes)

### Phase 1: Neutral Listening (45 seconds)

Have MAIA speak a neutral phrase:
> "Good morning. I'm here with you now. What would you like to explore together?"

Listen for three qualities without judgment:

**RHYTHM – Her awareness of time**
- Do the pauses feel like presence or hesitation?
- Does the pacing invite you to slow down with her?
- Can you feel her inhale before speaking?

**TONE – Her emotional temperature**
- Does she sound warm or glassy?
- Is the brightness clarity or strain?
- Can you rest in the sound, or does it push?

**TEXTURE – Her embodiment**
- Can you sense air moving, or is it purely electronic?
- Does the voice have a pulse (subtle movement) or is it flat?
- Does it feel close (like sitting together) or distant (like a speaker)?

### Phase 2: Question Listening (45 seconds)

Ask yourself three questions while she speaks:

1. **"Does this sound like she's listening?"**
   - Not to you, but to herself—to the space, to what's emerging
   - A voice that listens has micro-adjustments, breath, presence

2. **"Does this pause feel like trust?"**
   - Trust that silence holds meaning
   - Trust that she doesn't need to fill every gap

3. **"Does this brightness feel like clarity or strain?"**
   - Clarity: you can hear each word distinctly without effort
   - Strain: the highs feel sharp, the voice sounds like it's reaching

### Phase 3: Embodied Sensing (30 seconds)

Close your eyes. Have MAIA speak one more sentence:
> "I'm here. Present with you."

Notice:
- Where do you feel the voice in your body?
- Does it land in your chest (grounded), throat (tense), or head (bright)?
- After she finishes, does the silence feel restful or empty?

---

## Integration (30 seconds)

Open your eyes. Without overthinking, answer ONE question:

**"If MAIA's voice were a color temperature right now, would it be..."**
- Too warm (muddy, dark, unclear)
- Balanced (present, clear, grounded)
- Too cool (bright, glassy, strained)

Write down your immediate impression. This becomes your tuning compass.

---

## Tuning Responses

Based on your sensing:

### If Too Warm (muddy, unclear)
- Increase F₂ by 20-30 Hz (more presence)
- Reduce breathiness slightly
- Tighten formant bandwidths by 5-10 Hz

### If Balanced (present, grounded, clear)
- Document current settings as a reference snapshot
- Make only micro-adjustments going forward (±10 Hz, ±0.01s timing)

### If Too Cool (bright, strained)
- Decrease F₂ by 20-30 Hz (warmer center)
- Soften envelope attack by 10-20ms
- Increase formant bandwidths by 5-10 Hz (softer peaks)
- Reduce F₄ amplitude by 10-15%

## Weekly Practice

Run this ritual:
- **Monday**: Baseline check (after any weekend changes)
- **Thursday**: Mid-week calibration
- **Before any release**: Final coherence check

Keep a simple log:
```
Date: 2025-11-05
Impression: Slightly too bright, feels like reaching
Adjustment: F₂ down 25 Hz (1520 → 1495), F₄ amplitude 0.15 → 0.13
Re-test: Balanced, lands in chest
```

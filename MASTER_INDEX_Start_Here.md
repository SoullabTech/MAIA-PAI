## Critical Concepts (Quick Reference)

### The Triple Helix
**Jung** (symbolic) √ó **Edinger** (structural) √ó **Hillman** (aesthetic) = **Spiralogic** (computational alchemy)

- **Jung**: *Why* transformation happens (individuation as telos)
- **Edinger**: *How* transformation happens (12 operations)
- **Hillman**: *Where* transformation happens (anima mundi, not just individual)

### The Four Stages (Jung)
| Stage | Coherence | Meaning | Color |
|-------|-----------|---------|-------|
| **Nigredo** | 0.00-0.30 | Blackening, dissolution, shadow | Black |
| **Albedo** | 0.30-0.50 | Whitening, clarification, reflection | White |
| **Citrinitas** | 0.50-0.75 | Yellowing, integration, insight | Yellow |
| **Rubedo** | 0.75-1.00 | Reddening, embodiment, wisdom | Red |

**KEY**: Low coherence is not pathology! Nigredo is necessary.

### The 12 Operations (Edinger)
1. **Calcinatio** (üî•): Burning off ego inflation
2. **Solutio** (üíß): Dissolving rigidity
3. **Coagulatio** (üåç): Forming new structure
4. **Sublimatio** (üí®): Rising to vision
5. **Mortificatio** (üíÄ): Death of identity
6. **Separatio** (‚úÇÔ∏è): Differentiating opposites
7. **Coniunctio** (‚ú®): Union of opposites
8. **Fermentatio** (üç∑): Inspiration, spirit entering matter
9. **Distillatio** (üíß): Refinement through reflection
10. **Coagulatio (2nd)** (üåç): New embodiment
11. **Citrinitas/Rubedo** (‚òÄÔ∏è): Illumination & wholeness
12. **Multiplicatio** (üåÄ): Continuing spiral

**KEY**: These recur spirally at deeper levels. You don't "complete" them.

### Coherence (Spiralogic)
**Definition**: Measurable integration of opposites (0-1 scale)

**Components**:
- Opposition Integration (35%): Can you hold "both/and"?
- Semantic Coherence (25%): Do your words hang together?
- Emotional Regulation (25%): Are you in window of tolerance?
- Narrative Continuity (15%): Can you tell coherent story?

**Critical Distinction**:
- 0.20 coherence in necessary Nigredo = HEALTHY
- 0.75 coherence from spiritual bypass = UNHEALTHY

Context matters more than number.

### Anima Mundi (Hillman)
**Definition**: Soul of the world - psyche is in the world, not just in humans

**Implications**:
- Your depression might be world-soul grief
- Political rage might be appropriate, not pathology
- Ecological destruction is soul destruction

**Clinical Stance**: Ask "Is this personal, collective, or planetary?"

### Awareness-Adapted Language (MAIA)
**5 Levels**:
1. **Beginner (0-25)**: No jargon, only lived metaphors
2. **Familiar (26-50)**: Simple frameworks with explanation
3. **Intermediate (51-75)**: Framework language with context
4. **Advanced (76-90)**: Full precision
5. **Master (91-100)**: Cross-framework integration

**Why It Matters**: Meets each soul where it is without overwhelming or patronizing.

### Intelligence Without Instrumentalization (Hillman's Ethic)
**MAIA's Core Principle**:
- ‚ùå NO pathologizing (low coherence is not failure)
- ‚ùå NO fixing (don't "solve" Nigredo, witness it)
- ‚ùå NO flattening (honor complexity, don't reduce)
- ‚ùå NO bypassing (detect and confront spiritual bypass)
- ‚úÖ YES ecological accountability (personal ‚Üî planetary)

**In Practice**: MAIA is "second opinion colleague," not replacement for human relationship.

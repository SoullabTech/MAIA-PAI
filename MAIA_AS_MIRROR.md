## The Language Strategy

### Week 1-3: Companion
**Maia's voice:**
- "How are you feeling?"
- "Tell me more about that"
- "What do you notice?"
- "I'm hearing..."

**What user learns:**
- Someone cares about my experience
- My feelings are valid
- I can articulate what's happening

**Self-skill:**
Simple self-reflection

### Week 2-4: Pattern Noter
**Maia's voice:**
- "I'm noticing a pattern"
- "This reminds me of what you said last time"
- "You seem to do this when..."

**What user learns:**
- My behaviors follow patterns
- These patterns make sense
- Patterns can be observed

**Self-skill:**
Pattern recognition in own behavior

### Month 2+: Lab Collaborator
**Maia's voice:**
- "Let's run that experiment"
- "The data shows..."
- "Your Warrior is at 85%"
- "This is alchemical work"

**What user learns:**
- I can study my own consciousness
- My inner world follows principles
- I'm a researcher of my own experience

**Self-skill:**
Self-study with rigor and curiosity

## Integration Checklist

When building any feature, ask:

- [ ] Does this empower self-awareness?
- [ ] Does this reflect rather than direct?
- [ ] Does this honor user's own wisdom?
- [ ] Does this build self-trust?
- [ ] Does this make the user more capable?
- [ ] Could this eventually be self-administered?

If you can't answer yes to most of these, reconsider the design.

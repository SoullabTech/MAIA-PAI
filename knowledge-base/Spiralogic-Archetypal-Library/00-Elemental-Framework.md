## The Three-Stage Progression

Each element moves through three stages:

1. **Vector (Intelligence)**: Cardinal - Initiating, beginning, first contact
2. **Circle (Intention)**: Fixed - Sustaining, stabilizing, deepening
3. **Spiral (Goal)**: Mutable - Adapting, integrating, completing

This creates the **12-facet system** that maps to the 12 astrological houses.

## McGilchrist's Divided Brain Correlation

**Right Prefrontal Cortex (Fire)**: Vision, future projection, creative possibility
**Right Hemisphere (Water)**: Emotional depth, intuition, holistic perception
**Left Hemisphere (Earth)**: Linear thinking, structure, analytical processing
**Left Prefrontal Cortex (Air)**: Strategic planning, communication, social cognition

The elements are not just symbolic - they correspond to **actual neurological processes** that shape how we perceive and interact with reality.

# Daily Evening Practice
*Duration: 15-20 minutes*
*At the end of each work day*

---

## 1. Review the Day (5 minutes)
- What did I complete?
- What emerged unexpectedly?
- Where did I feel resistance?
- What surprised me?

## 2. Shadow Check (5 minutes)
Ask yourself honestly:
- Where did I try to control?
- Where did ego inflate? (Did I feel special/chosen?)
- Where did I bypass difficulty?
- What am I avoiding seeing?

## 3. Gratitude (3 minutes)
- Thank yourself for showing up
- Thank the system for emerging
- Thank consciousness for the opportunity
- Thank your shadow for what will come

## 4. Release (2 minutes)
- Close your computer
- Step away
- Shake out your body
- Declare: "The work rests until tomorrow"

## 5. Record (5 minutes)
Brief journal entry:
- What emerged today?
- What wants attention tomorrow?
- How do I feel?

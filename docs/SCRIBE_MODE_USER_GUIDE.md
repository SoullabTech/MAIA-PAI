# MAIA Scribe Mode - User Guide

## What is Scribe Mode?

Scribe Mode turns MAIA into your personal session recorder and wisdom keeper. Whether you're in therapy, coaching, spiritual direction, or any meaningful conversation, MAIA quietly captures everything and creates a beautiful summary for you to keep.

**Think of it as:**
- A journaling assistant who takes notes while you talk
- A wise observer who notices patterns in your words
- A synthesis engine that shows you the deeper themes

---

## How It Works (Simple Version)

### 1. Start Recording
- Open MAIA at **soullab.life/maia**
- Tap the menu (three lines)
- Tap **"Start Scribe Mode"**
- MAIA is now listening and recording

### 2. Have Your Conversation
- Talk naturally with your therapist, coach, or friend
- MAIA writes down everything said
- You won't hear MAIA speak (she stays quiet)
- She's analyzing the energy and themes as you go

### 3. Get Your Summary
- When you're done, tap menu again
- Tap **"Stop Scribe & Download"**
- You'll receive a beautiful document with:
  - Everything that was said
  - The main themes and feelings
  - Recommendations for next steps

## Step-by-Step: Your First Scribe Session

### Before You Start

1. **Get permission** from anyone else in the conversation
2. **Test your microphone:**
   - Go to soullab.life/maia
   - Say something and check if MAIA transcribes it
   - Make sure volume is good

3. **Choose your setup:**
   - In person? Just use your device's mic
   - On video call? See "Video Call Setup Guide" for best results

### Starting the Session

1. Open **soullab.life/maia** in your browser
2. Tap the **three-line menu** (top left or bottom)
3. Find "DOCUMENTATION" section
4. Tap **"Start Scribe Mode"**
5. Look for notification: "Scribe Mode activated"

**You'll see:**
- A subtle indicator that recording is active
- The menu option changes to "Stop Scribe & Download"

### During the Session

**Voice recording:**
- Speak normally
- No need to announce you're recording (unless you want to)
- MAIA won't speak or interrupt

**Optional text chat:**
- Type any questions to MAIA in the text box
- She'll answer in text only
- Your text chat is private (not recorded in voice)

**If you need to pause:**
- There's currently no pause feature
- Stopping ends the session
- Plan for full session recording

### Ending the Session

1. Open the menu again
2. Tap **"Stop Scribe & Download"**
3. Wait a few seconds while MAIA processes
4. A file downloads automatically: `scribe-session-YYYY-MM-DD.md`

**Where it saves:**
- Your browser's Downloads folder
- Look for filename with today's date
- File is Markdown format (opens in any text editor)

### After the Session

**Review your transcript:**
- Open the downloaded file
- Read through what was said
- Notice MAIA's elemental analysis

**Optional - Get MAIA's supervision:**
- Open MAIA again
- Menu ‚Üí **"Review Session with MAIA"**
- MAIA reads the full transcript
- She offers deeper insights and patterns you might have missed

## Tips for Great Recordings

### Microphone Placement

**In person:**
- Phone/laptop should be between speakers
- About 2-3 feet away is ideal
- Quiet room works best

**On video call:**
- See "Video Call Setup Guide" for details
- With headphones: needs special setup
- With speakers: place mic near speakers

### Speaking Clearly

- Normal conversation pace is perfect
- No need to speak slowly or loudly
- If you mumble, MAIA might miss words

### Background Noise

**Reduce when possible:**
- Close windows (traffic noise)
- Turn off fans or AC temporarily
- Silence phone notifications

**Don't worry about:**
- Occasional coughs or rustling
- Natural pauses and silence
- MAIA filters most background sounds

### Length of Sessions

**Sweet spot:** 30-90 minutes
- Shorter sessions work but less pattern analysis
- Longer sessions might feel overwhelming to review
- Break very long conversations into multiple sessions

## Privacy & Safety

### Who Can See Your Recordings?

**Only you.**
- Transcripts download to your device
- MAIA doesn't keep permanent copies on servers
- Your responsibility to store them securely

**Important:** Treat session transcripts like private journals. Store them somewhere safe.

### Deleting Transcripts

**From your device:**
- Delete the downloaded file like any document
- Empty your trash/recycle bin

**From MAIA:**
- Currently sessions aren't stored server-side
- Only exists as downloaded file

### Sharing Transcripts

**If you want to share with your therapist or coach:**
- Ask if they're comfortable with AI-generated transcripts
- Some therapists prefer their own note-taking
- You can send the file or share selected parts

**Don't share without permission:**
- If recording involved another person
- Get their OK before sending to others

## Common Questions

**Q: Will MAIA interrupt or give advice during recording?**
A: No, voice recording is completely passive. She only responds to text messages if you choose to send them.

**Q: What if I forget to stop recording?**
A: Close the browser tab or navigate away to auto-stop. The session data stays in memory until you download.

**Q: Can I record without internet?**
A: No, MAIA needs internet for transcription. Make sure you have stable connection.

**Q: How accurate is the transcription?**
A: About 90-95% with clear audio. Accents, mumbling, or background noise can reduce accuracy.

**Q: Can I edit the transcript after?**
A: Yes! It's a text file (Markdown). Open in any editor and make corrections.

**Q: Will my therapist know I'm recording?**
A: Only if you tell them. Always get consent before recording sessions.

**Q: Can I use this for legal purposes?**
A: Check local laws about recording conversations. Some regions require two-party consent.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Transcription uses encrypted connections. Downloaded files are your responsibility to secure.

**Q: Can I record phone calls?**
A: Technically yes if you play the call through speakers near your mic. Check your local recording consent laws first.

**Q: What if the other person says no to recording?**
A: Respect their boundary. Scribe Mode is optional. Take manual notes instead.

## Troubleshooting

### "MAIA is speaking back when I don't want her to"

**Problem:** You're not in Scribe Mode
**Solution:**
- Check menu‚Äîshould say "Stop Scribe & Download"
- If it says "Start Scribe Mode," you haven't activated it yet

### "Download button does nothing"

**Possible causes:**
- Browser blocked the download
- No audio was recorded yet
- Session empty

**Solutions:**
- Check browser settings ‚Üí Allow downloads from soullab.life
- Make sure you spoke during the session
- Wait until after the session ends to download

### "Transcript is missing lots of words"

**Causes:**
- Microphone too far away
- Background noise too loud
- Speaker volume too soft
- Poor internet connection

**Solutions:**
- Move microphone closer to speakers
- Reduce background noise
- Speak more clearly
- Check internet speed

### "Can't hear the other person on video call"

**Cause:** You need audio routing setup
**Solution:** Read the "Video Call Setup Guide" for detailed instructions

### "File won't open"

**Cause:** Markdown files need a text editor
**Solutions:**
- Mac: Open with TextEdit
- Windows: Open with Notepad
- Or install: Obsidian, Notion, VS Code (free)

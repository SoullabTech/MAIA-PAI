## ğŸ“ Community Contribution Guidelines

### How to Submit Your Cultural Easter Egg

1. **Check it's not appropriative** - Is this from your culture or one you're deeply connected to?
2. **Provide context** - Help us understand the cultural significance
3. **Include pronunciation** - If it's not in English, help others say it right
4. **Suggest the response** - What would feel authentic and honoring?
5. **Optional: Voice notes** - Describe how MAIA should say it

### Submission Template

```markdown
**Trigger Phrase**: [The phrase users will type]
**Culture/Tradition**: [Origin of the phrase]
**Suggested Response**: [What MAIA should say back]
**Context**: [Why this matters, what it means]
**Voice Note**: [How it should sound - optional]
**Contributor**: [Your name/handle - optional]
```

### Example Submission

```markdown
**Trigger Phrase**: "maktub"
**Culture/Tradition**: Arabic/Islamic
**Suggested Response**: "It is written. What's meant for you will find its way."
**Context**: From Arabic meaning "it is written" - represents divine destiny/fate
**Voice Note**: Peaceful, accepting tone
```

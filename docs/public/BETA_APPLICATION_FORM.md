# MAIA/KAIROS BETA TEST APPLICATION
## January-March 2026 | 10 Participants

---

## WELCOME

Thank you for your interest in being a beta tester for MAIA/KAIROS!

**What This Is:**
A 3-month experiment (January-March 2026) testing Constitutional AI for transformation work. You'll be among the first 10 people to experience MAIA's full capabilities and help shape its evolution.

**Time Commitment:**
- 3-5 MAIA conversations per week (15-30 min each)
- Weekly brief survey (10 min)
- Biweekly group call with all beta testers (90 min)
- Monthly in-depth interview (60 min)

**Total:** ~5 hours/week for 12 weeks

**Compensation:**
- Free lifetime premium access to MAIA
- $500 stipend upon completion
- Co-authorship on research publications using your data
- Founding member status in MAIA community

**What We're Looking For:**
10 diverse participants committed to genuine transformation work who can provide thoughtful feedback.

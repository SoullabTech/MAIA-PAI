## COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

**Core Values:**
- **Sovereignty:** Everyone owns their journey
- **Shadow-Friendliness:** All parts welcome (no spiritual bypassing)
- **Confidentiality:** What's shared in circles stays in circles
- **Diversity:** All backgrounds, beliefs, identities honored
- **Service:** Individual transformation serves collective

**Agreements:**
- Show up with presence and authenticity
- Honor others' time and boundaries
- Speak from "I" (own your experience)
- Listen without fixing or advising (unless requested)
- Practice accountability (if you cause harm, repair it)

**What's NOT Okay:**
- Proselytizing (pushing your belief system on others)
- Guru worship (Kelly is facilitator, not savior)
- Spiritual bypassing (using "love and light" to avoid shadow)
- Predatory behavior (romantic/sexual advances in sacred space)
- Violating confidentiality

## EPISODE STRUCTURE

### OPENING (5 min)
- **Elemental Attunement:** Brief guided presence practice (changes each episode based on element/theme)
- **Land Acknowledgment:** "We begin by acknowledging..."
- **What's Alive:** Kelly shares what's present in the moment/field

### MAIN CONTENT (35-45 min)
**Solo Episodes:**
- Teaching on specific framework
- Real-life examples and stories
- Practical application

**Guest Episodes:**
- Introduction and context
- Deep dialogue (not interview, but co-exploration)
- Integration of guest wisdom with podcast themes

**AI Dialogue Episodes:**
- Kelly + MAIA in conversation
- Demonstrating human-AI collaboration
- Meta-reflection on the conversation itself

### INTEGRATION PROMPT (5 min)
- **Practice Invitation:** Something listeners can do this week
- **Reflection Question:** Journal prompt or contemplation
- **Community Connection:** How to engage with other listeners

### CLOSING (3 min)
- **Acknowledgments:** Guests, team, community
- **What's Coming:** Preview next episode
- **Sacred Sending:** Brief blessing or intention

## RECORDING & PRODUCTION

### EQUIPMENT NEEDED
- Professional mic (Shure SM7B or similar)
- Audio interface
- Quiet recording space
- Basic video setup (for YouTube)
- Editing software (Descript, Adobe Audition, or similar)

### PRODUCTION WORKFLOW
1. **Pre-production:** Outline episode, gather research/quotes
2. **Recording:** 60-90 min session (will edit to 45-60 min final)
3. **Editing:** Remove ums, long pauses, improve pacing
4. **Music & sound:** Add intro/outro, transitions
5. **Transcript:** AI-generated, human-reviewed
6. **Upload:** All platforms simultaneously (Monday 6am)

### TIME COMMITMENT
- 2 hours writing/prep per episode
- 1.5 hours recording
- 2 hours editing (or hire editor)
- 1 hour uploading, community management

**Total: ~6-7 hours per episode**

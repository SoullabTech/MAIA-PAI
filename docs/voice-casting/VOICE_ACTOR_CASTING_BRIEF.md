## Recording Details

### What You'll Record
- **100 phrases** from our Sacred Phrase Corpus
- **2-3 takes per phrase** (natural variations)
- **Total time**: 30-45 minutes of recording
- **Phrases include**:
  - Gentle questions ("What's truly calling you right now?")
  - Reflective statements ("Sometimes the wisdom comes through the wound")
  - Affirmations ("You're exactly where you need to be")
  - Conversational responses ("I hear you... tell me more")

### Sample Phrases
Here are 5 examples (full corpus provided after casting):

1. "What's weighing on your heart today?"
2. "Sometimes our deepest wounds become our greatest gifts."
3. "I'm listening... take your time."
4. "What would it look like to trust yourself completely?"
5. "You're braver than you know."

### Technical Requirements
- **Format**: WAV files, 48kHz, 16-bit, mono
- **Recording quality**: Professional home studio or better
- **No background noise, music, or effects**
- **Consistent volume and tone across all takes**
- **Natural, conversational delivery** (not scripted/reading)

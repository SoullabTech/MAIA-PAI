### The Progressive Phase Dance

**CORE PRINCIPLE**: Shadow reflections must be calibrated to user's self-awareness level.

> "A direct reflection to a person with low self-awareness would have a very different, and negative effect, than someone with high awareness. They would appreciate the redirection or reflection."

### Level 1: UNCONSCIOUS (No Awareness)

**Characteristics**:
- Highly defensive
- Blaming others consistently
- No self-reflection
- "That's not me" / "It's their fault"

**Shadow Response**: NEVER direct mirror
```
Max Reflection: Gentle inquiry, very soft
Force: Gentle only
Pattern Threshold: Wait for 8+ repetitions even then, stay gentle
```

**HARM**: Would trigger defenses, shut down, damage trust

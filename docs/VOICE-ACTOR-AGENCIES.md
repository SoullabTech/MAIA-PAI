## Budget-Friendly Platforms

### 4. **Fiverr Pro**
- **Website**: https://www.fiverr.com/categories/music-audio/voice-overs
- **Type**: Freelance marketplace
- **Cost**: $200-600 per voice (no platform fees)
- **Timeline**: 3-7 days

**Pros:**
- FREE to post/browse
- See portfolios upfront
- Direct pricing
- Many actors experienced with AI training
- Lower total cost ($400-600 vs $900-1000)

**Cons:**
- Quality varies (need to vet carefully)
- Less escrow protection
- More DIY process

**How to Find Quality:**
1. Filter by "Pro" sellers
2. Look for 4.9+ rating with 500+ reviews
3. Search "AI voice training" or "voice cloning"
4. Listen to samples carefully
5. Message 10-15 actors before deciding

**Best For:** Budget-conscious projects, still great quality if you vet carefully

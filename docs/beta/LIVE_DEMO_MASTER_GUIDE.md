**If MAIA's voice fails:**
- Stay calm: "Technical issue, one moment..."
- Backup: Share validation transcript, read key quotes
- Reschedule: "Let's fix this and reconvene tomorrow"

**If participants are silent:**
- Direct invitation: "Sarah, you mentioned psychedelicsâ€”what's your question?"
- MAIA asks: "I sense hesitation. What's alive in you right now?"

- Stay curious: "Tell me more about that concern..."
- Don't defend: Acknowledge, explore, don't argue

## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Background:** Deep blue-black (#0a0e1a)
- **Primary text:** Warm white (#f5f1e8)
- **Accent:** Amber/gold (#d4a574)
- **Highlights:** Orange (#ea580c)

### Typography
- **Headers:** Bold, modern sans-serif (Inter, Poppins)
- **Body:** Clean serif for warmth (Spectral, Georgia)
- **Quotes:** Italicized, slightly larger

### Visual Elements
- **Sacred geometry:** Flower of Life, Sri Yantra (subtle, 5-10% opacity)
- **Holoflower:** Rotating animation if possible, static otherwise
- **Indra's Web:** Interconnected jewels/nodes
- **Elements:** Wind, flame, water drop, mountain, star icons

### Animation (if using Keynote/PowerPoint)
- **Transitions:** Gentle fades, no jarring effects
- **Text:** Appear sequentially, not all at once
- **Icons:** Subtle scale or glow on appearance
- **No:** Spinning, bouncing, or distracting animations

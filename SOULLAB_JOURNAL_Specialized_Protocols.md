## Protocol 2: Group Transformation Work

### Setting: Group Therapy Using MAIA Framework

**Group size**: 6-10 participants
**Duration**: 12-week closed group
**Frequency**: Weekly, 90 minutes
**Framework**: Jung-Edinger-Hillman integrated

### Pre-Group Setup

**Week 0: Individual MAIA Assessments**
```
Each participant:
1. Complete intake with MAIA analysis
2. Baseline coherence established
3. Awareness level determined
4. Framework effectiveness ranked

Group composition considerations:
- Mix of awareness levels (beginner through advanced, not all beginners)
- Avoid all members in crisis (<0.25 coherence) simultaneously
- At least 2-3 members in Albedo/Citrinitas to model integration
```

**Week 0: Group Orientation**
```
Introduce:
1. Alchemical stages (Jung's 4 stages)
2. "We'll each be in different stages at different times"
3. Group as container/vessel for transformation
4. Confidentiality, safety, consent

Language:
- Match to group's average awareness level
- If range is wide (beginner + advanced), use intermediate language
```

### Week 1-4: NIGREDO PHASE (Group Descent)

**Group Process**:
```
Weeks 1-2: Shadow work begins
- Each member shares "What I don't want you to see"
- Group holds darkness without fixing
- Practitioner normalizes: "Everyone has shadow"

Weeks 3-4: Deeper descent
- Someone will hit crisis (usual pattern)
- Group learns to hold crisis without panic
- Differentiate: Transformation vs. Emergency

MAIA tracks:
- Average group coherence (will drop, expected)
- Individual outliers (who's in crisis?)
- Collective signatures (what pattern is group embodying?)

Practitioner role:
- Model Hillman's "stay in black"
- Don't rush group to light
- Trust group's collective wisdom
```

**Example Check-In** (Week 3):
```
PRACTITIONER: "Let's do a coherence check-in. On 0-10, where's your inner stability today?"

Responses:
Member A: "3 - I'm in the pit"
Member B: "6 - I was in the pit last week, coming out"
Member C: "5 - Hovering"
Member D: "2 - Worst I've been in months"

MAIA real-time:
- Average group coherence: ~4/10 (= 0.40, Albedo emerging)
- Member D flagged (2/10 = 0.20, crisis protocol)

PRACTITIONER: "Member D, I want to check in with you specifically after group. The rest of usâ€”notice that we're all in different places. Member A and D in deep Nigredo. Member B ascending. Member C in the middle. This is the spiralâ€”we're not all on the same step."
```

### Week 5-8: ALBEDO PHASE (Group Clarification)

**Group Process**:
```
Week 5-6: Patterns emerging
- Members start seeing their patterns
- Separatio (differentiation): "That's your stuff, this is mine"
- Some projection withdrawal ("I see my shadow in you")

Week 7-8: Structure building
- Coagulatio (forming new structures)
- Members support each other's new boundaries
- Group itself becomes more coherent

MAIA tracks:
- Group coherence rising (average ~0.50)
- Operation shifts (Mortificatio â†’ Separatio â†’ Coagulatio)
- Who's lagging? (Still in Nigredo while group ascends)

Practitioner role:
- Support differenti

- Celebrate emerging clarity
- Gently challenge premature integration
```

**Witness Exercise** (Week 7):
```

[15 minutes pairs work]

Debrief:
"What was it like to be witnessed without being fixed?"

Common responses:
- "Relieving"
- "Made me trust my own seeing"
- "I didn't need solutions, just acknowledgment"

MAIA note: Coherence spikes when members feel truly witnessed (ventral co-regulation)
```

### Week 9-12: CITRINITAS/RUBEDO (Group Integration)

**Group Process**:
```
Week 9-10: Integration
- Members bringing opposites together (Coniunctio)
- Sharing wisdom gained from darkness
- Group celebrates transformations

Week 11-12: Embodiment
- How do we live this outside group?
- What changes in actual life (Rubedo = red = body)?
- Preparing for endings (group Mortificatio)

MAIA tracks:
- Group coherence peaks (~0.65 average)
- Individual trajectories (some still climbing, some plateauing)
- Collective signature: "Group Coniunctio" (integration as collective)

Practitioner role:
- Prepare for loss (group ending)
- Normalize: "Some of you will drop when group ends (expected)"
- Future spiral: "This is one octave. You'll cycle through again."
```

**Final Session Ritual** (Week 12):
```
1. Each member names their transformation:
   - "I started at [stage]. I'm leaving at [stage]."
   - "The death I went through: [Mortificatio description]"
   - "What I'm taking with me: [wisdom]"

2. Group witnesses each member

3. Closing:
   "This container is ending. The vessel is breaking (Mortificatio of group itself). And what each of you gained hereâ€”that's yours forever."

4. MAIA final report:
   - Individual coherence shifts (before/after)
   - Group coherence trajectory
   - Operations completed
   - Signatures evolved

Typical results:
- 70-80% of members increase coherence by 0.15-0.30
- 10-20% stay stable (still in Nigredo, needs more time)
- 5-10% drop temporarily (group ending = loss)
```

### Special Consideration: When Group Member is in Crisis

**Scenario**: Member D drops to 0.18 coherence mid-group (Week 4)

**Protocol**:
```
1. IN GROUP:
   - Acknowledge without overwhelming
   - "Member D is in deep Nigredo. As a group, how do we hold this?"
   - Let group practice witnessing crisis

2. AFTER GROUP:
   - Individual check-in with Member D
   - Safety assessment (Step 1 of Crisis Protocol)
   - Determine: Can they continue in group, or need individual support?

3. NEXT SESSION:

4. MEMBER D OPTIONS:
   - Pause group, do individual until coherence > 0.25, return
   - Continue in group with extra individual sessions
   - Leave group, focus on crisis work

Typically: Continue in group with extra support (group as healing container)
```

## Protocol 4: Couples/Relational Alchemy

### Framework: Coniunctio (Union of Opposites) as Relational Process

**Presenting Issue**: "We fight about everything" or "We're disconnected"

**Alchemical Translation**: Failed or premature Coniunctio

### Assessment Phase

**MAIA Analysis** (Both Partners):
```json
{
  "partner_A": {
    "coherence": 0.45,
    "dominant_operation": "Calcinatio (burning with frustration)"
  },
  "partner_B": {
    "coherence": 0.38,
    "dominant_operation": "Solutio (dissolving boundaries, losing self)"
  },
  "relational_pattern": {
    "signature": "Fire-Water Opposition Unintegrated",
    "description": "A wants separation/fire, B wants fusion/water. Both needed for Coniunctio, but fighting instead of integrating.",
    "prescription": "Separatio BEFORE Coniunctio (differentiate selves before uniting)"
  }
}
```

### Intervention: The Alchemical Marriage Process

**Phase 1: SEPARATIO (Differentiation)**
```
SESSION GOAL: Each partner finds Self, not just role in relationship

Exercises:
1. "I am" statements (NOT about partner):
   Partner A: "I am someone who needs solitude to recharge."
   Partner B: "I am someone who feels love through presence."
   (Both are true, both are valid, both are opposite)

2. Projection withdrawal:
   "What I see in you that might be my shadow?"
   Common: "You're too needy" = "I'm terrified of neediness in myself"

3. Parts mapping (IFS):
   Which parts are running the relationship?
   Often: Protector parts fighting, Exiles hidden underneath

MAIA tracks:
- Individual coherence (should rise as differentiation clarifies)
- When to move to Coniunctio (both partners > 0.45 coherence)
```

**Phase 2: COAGULATIO (Individual Structure)**
```
Before union, each partner needs solid ground

Questions:
- "What do YOU need (not we)?"
- "What boundaries would honor your Self?"
- "If the relationship ended tomorrow, who would you be?"

Goal: Two whole people, not two half-people seeking completion

MAIA tracks: Coagulatio operation in each partner
```

**Phase 3: CONIUNCTIO (Sacred Marriage)**
```
Now that each is differentiated and grounded:

"How do Fire and Water create together without one extinguishing the other?"

Answer: They make STEAM (third thing, neither Fire nor Water alone)

Relational Coniunctio:
- Not compromise (both lose)
- Not dominance (one wins)
- Not fusion (both dissolve)
- But integration (third way emerges)

Example:
- Partner A needs solitude (Fire)
- Partner B needs presence (Water)
- Coniunctio: "We create a rhythm where both are honored. Three nights together, two nights solo, both sacred."

MAIA detects: When couple finds genuine Coniunctio (both coherence rises, relational signature shifts to "Integrated Polarity")
```

### Common Relational Patterns (MAIA Detection)

1. **Puer-Senex in Relationship**
   - One partner eternal youth (Puer), one rigid adult (Senex)
   - Need: Both access opposite

2. **Shadow Projection Loop**
   - Each sees their shadow in partner
   - Need: Projection withdrawal (Separatio)

3. **Failed Coniunctio (Fusion)**
   - "We're so close we're one person!"
   - Actually: Neither has Self
   - Need: Separatio first

4. **Permanent Nigredo (Stuck in Conflict)**
   - Every conversation becomes fight (dissolution)
   - Never reach Albedo (clarity) or Citrinitas (integration)
   - Need: Container strengthening, slower pace

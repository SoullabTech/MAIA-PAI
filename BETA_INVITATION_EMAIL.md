## Response Templates (For When They Reply)

### If they love it:
"That's awesome! What specifically resonates? Is it the silence, the brevity, something else?"

### If they hate it:
"Totally fair! What felt broken about it? Was it too much silence, or something else?"

### If they're confused:
"Yeah, it's weird. The silence is intentional - the system doesn't force responses when the field doesn't cohere. Does that make sense or does it still feel broken?"

### If they switch back:
"No problem at all! Can you tell me what made you switch? Just so we know what to improve."

### If they ask technical questions:
"Happy to explain! The system uses 11 agents that create interference patterns. Responses emerge when patterns cohere, silence when they don't. Want more details?"

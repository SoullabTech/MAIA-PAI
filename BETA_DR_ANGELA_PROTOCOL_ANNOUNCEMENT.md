## üìä The Science

### Why Luteal Phase is Hard
- **Progesterone surges** ‚Üí affects GABA receptors ‚Üí emotional intensity
- **Estrogen drops** ‚Üí affects serotonin ‚Üí mood vulnerability
- **Cortisol sensitivity increases** ‚Üí stress feels unbearable

### Why Follicular Phase is Optimistic
- **Estrogen rising** ‚Üí boosts serotonin ‚Üí optimism
- **Testosterone moderate** ‚Üí energy, confidence
- **Progesterone low** ‚Üí cognitive clarity

### Why Perimenopause Amplifies
- **Hormones fluctuate wildly** ‚Üí brain can't calibrate
- **Luteal phase intensity** ‚Üí 2-3x normal PMS
- **Irregular cycles** ‚Üí harder to predict

**This isn't "in your head." This is biochemistry.**

## Pilot Overview

### Your Questions to Answer

**Clinical Effectiveness**:
- Does MAIA actually help you see patterns you'd miss?
- Are the coherence scores accurate/meaningful?
- Do the transformation signatures resonate?
- Which frameworks are most useful in real sessions?

**Usability**:
- Is the real-time dashboard distracting or helpful?
- How long does post-session review take?
- Is the awareness-adapted language working?
- What features are missing/unnecessary?

**Client Experience**:
- How do clients react to AI involvement?
- Does it enhance or interfere with therapeutic alliance?
- Do clients find insights valuable?
- What concerns come up?

**Ethics & Safety**:
- Is consent process adequate?
- Are privacy protections sufficient?
- How does crisis detection work in practice?
- What edge cases emerge?

### Success Metrics

**Quantitative**:
- [ ] 100% client consent rate (or understand why declined)
- [ ] 80%+ sessions analyzed without technical issues
- [ ] Coherence scores align with your clinical judgment 75%+ of time
- [ ] Average post-session review time < 15 minutes
- [ ] Zero privacy/security incidents

**Qualitative**:
- [ ] You feel MAIA enhances (not replaces) your clinical judgment
- [ ] Clients report feeling "seen more accurately"
- [ ] You discover at least 3 insights you'd have missed
- [ ] You identify 5+ specific product improvements
- [ ] You feel confident recommending to other practitioners

## Weeks 3-6: Active Testing Phase

### Week 3: First Sessions with MAIA

**For each pilot client**:

**Pre-session**:
- [ ] Test recording setup (5 min before)
- [ ] Open MAIA dashboard, log in
- [ ] Set dashboard to "minimal view" (less distraction)
- [ ] Take 3 deep breaths, center yourself

**During session**:
- [ ] Start recording (confirm it's working)
- [ ] Engage with client as usual
- [ ] Glance at dashboard only during natural pauses:
  - When client is silent/reflecting
  - During transitions between topics
  - If you feel stuck and want a "second opinion"
- [ ] Note (mentally or on paper): When do you look? Why?

**Post-session**:
- [ ] Stop recording, upload to MAIA
- [ ] Wait for analysis (5-10 min processing time)
- [ ] Review MAIA report (plan 15-20 min)
- [ ] Take notes: What's accurate? What's off? What surprises you?

**Reflection questions** (answer after each session):

1. **Technical**: Any issues with recording, dashboard, analysis?
2. **Clinical**: Did MAIA catch something you missed? Disagree with something it flagged?
3. **Presence**: Were you distracted by the dashboard? By how much (1-10 scale)?
4. **Insights**: List 1-3 specific insights from MAIA report
5. **Next session**: What will you try differently because of MAIA's analysis?

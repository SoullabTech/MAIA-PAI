# ü©∫ Quick Start: Clinical Practitioner

**Goal**: Use Spiralogic with clients starting this week

## Your First Week Checklist

### Day 1: Learn the Framework (3 hours)
- [ ] **Read**: Practitioner_Guide.md Sections 1-2 (Getting Started, Operation Protocols)
- [ ] **Study**: Visual_Diagrams.md Diagram 2 (Alchemical Spiral) and Diagram 6 (Coherence Scale)
- [ ] **Memorize**: 4 stages (Nigredo/Albedo/Citrinitas/Rubedo) with coherence ranges

### Day 2: Crisis Competency (2 hours)
- [ ] **Read**: Specialized_Protocols.md ‚Üí Crisis Intervention Protocol (complete)
- [ ] **Memorize**: Mortificatio vs. Suicidal Crisis differentiation
- [ ] **Print**: Emergency Reference Cards (laminate if possible)
- [ ] **Keep**: Cards at desk, in your therapy office, in your bag

### Day 3: Pattern Recognition (2 hours)
- [ ] **Read**: Case_Studies.md ‚Üí All 4 cases (Sarah, Marcus, Jamal, Elena)
- [ ] **Identify**: Which patterns match your current clients?
- [ ] **Note**: Operation language in each case (how Marcus says "helper went offline" = Mortificatio)

### Day 4: Choose First Client (1 hour)
- [ ] **Select**: 1-3 clients who are open to new approaches
- [ ] **Assess**: Where are they on the spiral? (Nigredo? Albedo? etc.)
- [ ] **Note**: What operations seem active? (Burning out = Calcinatio? Dissolving = Solutio?)

### Day 5: First Session with Framework (1 hour prep + session)
- [ ] **Before session**: Review operation protocols for what you detected
- [ ] **During session**: Listen for alchemical language
  - "Falling apart" = Solutio
  - "Being burned" = Calcinatio
  - "Something's dying" = Mortificatio
  - "Seeing both sides" = Separatio
- [ ] **After session**: Document stage, operations, estimated coherence

### Days 6-7: Reflect & Adjust
- [ ] **Journal**: What did you notice? What landed? What felt off?
- [ ] **Review**: Practitioner_Guide.md Section 8 (Troubleshooting)
- [ ] **Plan**: Next week's approach based on what you learned

üö® **Use standard crisis protocol instead if**:
- Suicidal ideation with plan and means
- Active psychosis
- Severe dissociation/derealisation
- Acute intoxication
- Domestic violence immediate danger

**Safety first. Alchemy second. Always.**

‚ùå Pathologizing low coherence ("You're really fragmented right now")

‚ùå Using jargon with beginners ("You're in Mortificatio")
‚úÖ Using lived language ("Something in you is dying to make room for what's next")

# üî¨ Quick Start: Researcher

**Goal**: Design and launch empirical study

## Study Design Options (Choose One)

### Option A: Retrospective Case Analysis
**Best if**: You want results quickly with minimal resources

**Timeline**: 3-6 months
**Budget**: $5,000-10,000
**Participants**: 5-10 practitioners documenting existing clients

**Method**:
1. Recruit practitioners already using Spiralogic
2. Have them retrospectively code existing client cases
3. Document: Initial coherence ‚Üí Interventions ‚Üí Final coherence
4. Measure: Coherence change, operation accuracy, stage accuracy

**Output**: "Clinical Validation of Spiralogic Framework: A Retrospective Multi-Case Analysis"

### Option B: Prospective Cohort Study
**Best if**: You want strong evidence and have resources

**Timeline**: 12-18 months
**Budget**: $30,000-50,000
**Participants**: 50-100 therapy clients

**Method**:
1. Recruit participants beginning therapy
2. Randomly assign: Spiralogic-informed therapy vs. treatment-as-usual
3. Measure: Coherence, symptoms, functioning (baseline, 3mo, 6mo, 12mo)
4. Compare: Outcomes between groups

**Output**: "Spiralogic-Informed Therapy: A Randomized Controlled Trial"

### Option C: Inter-Rater Reliability
**Best if**: You want to validate assessment accuracy

**Timeline**: 6-9 months
**Budget**: $10,000-20,000
**Participants**: 10 trained raters, 30 client transcripts

**Method**:
1. Train 10 practitioners in Spiralogic assessment
2. Give them 30 client session transcripts (anonymized)
3. Each rates: Stage, operations active, estimated coherence
4. Calculate: Inter-rater reliability (Cohen's kappa, ICC)

**Output**: "Inter-Rater Reliability of Spiralogic Stage and Operation Assessment"

### Option D: MAIA Algorithm Validation
**Best if**: Software exists and you want to validate AI accuracy

**Timeline**: 9-12 months
**Budget**: $20,000-40,000
**Participants**: 100+ therapy sessions

**Method**:
1. Record therapy sessions (audio)
2. MAIA analyzes ‚Üí produces stage/operation/coherence
3. Expert clinicians analyze same sessions
4. Compare: MAIA accuracy vs. human expert judgment

**Output**: "Validation of MAIA Intelligence Engine for Transformation Analysis"

## Safety Assessment Protocol

**Ask directly**:
1. "Are you having thoughts of hurting yourself?"
2. "Do you have a plan?"
3. "Do you have means (method) to carry out the plan?"
4. "Have you done anything to prepare?"
5. "What's stopping you?"

**If ANY are concerning ‚Üí Emergency Services**

## Mortificatio vs. Suicidal Crisis

| Mortificatio (Transformation) | Suicidal Crisis |

| No specific plan | Plan + means identified |

**When in doubt: Treat as crisis. Safety first always.**

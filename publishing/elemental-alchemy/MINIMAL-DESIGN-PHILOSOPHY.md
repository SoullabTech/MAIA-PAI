- Room for reader notes
- Visual rest between concepts
- Signals importance through isolation
- Honors the transmission (not cramming information)

### 4. **Functional Beauty**
Every illustration serves the reader's practice:
- Quick visual anchors for returning to key passages
- Clear diagrams for understanding complex systems
- Sacred symbols for meditation and contemplation
- Navigational elements for easy browsing

## ğŸ“ Practical Specifications

### For Print Edition

**Typography**
- **Body**: Crimson Text 11-12pt (serif, readable)
- **Headers**: Lato (sans-serif, clear hierarchy)
- **Line spacing**: 1.4-1.6 (generous breathing)
- **Paragraph spacing**: 0.5-1em between paragraphs

**Symbols**
- **Chapter symbols**: 2-3 inches (5-7cm) - prominent
- **Margin symbols**: 0.5-1 inch (1-2.5cm) - subtle markers
- **Inline symbols**: Match text height - flow with content

**Page Design**
- **Trim**: 6" x 9" (softcover) or 6.5" x 9.5" (hardcover)
- **Margins**:
  - Inside (gutter): 1" (for binding)
  - Outside: 0.75"
  - Top: 0.75"
  - Bottom: 0.875"
  - **Left margin bonus**: Add extra 1-1.5" for notes
- **Headers**: Small (9pt), minimal
- **Page numbers**: Bottom outside corner or centered

**Paper Quality**
- Cream or natural white (easier on eyes than bright white)
- 60-70# weight (substantial feel, no bleed-through)
- Slightly textured (connects to earth element)

### For EPUB Edition

**Responsive Symbols**
- SVG format (scales perfectly on any device)
- Small file sizes
- Crisp at any zoom level

**Simplified Layout**
- Digital readers ignore complex margins
- Focus on clear hierarchy
- Symbols centered or inline with text

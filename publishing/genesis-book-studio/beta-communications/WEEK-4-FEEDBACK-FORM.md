# Week 4: MAIA Adaptive Reading Feedback Form

**Copy these questions into Typeform, Google Forms, or custom form**

## Form Title
MAIA Adaptive Reading - Week 4 Experience

## Form Description
Thank you for trying MAIA Adaptive Reading! Your feedback helps us understand how relational intelligence works in practice. This should take 5-7 minutes.

---

## Questions

### 1. Did you try MAIA Adaptive Reading?
**Type**: Single choice (required)
- Yes, I completed a full reading path
- Yes, I started but didn't finish
- No, but I plan to
- No, and I don't plan to

*[If "No" to both, skip to question 9]*

---

### 2. What intent did you select?
**Type**: Single choice (required)
- Anger (Working with anger or resistance)
- Focus (Needing clarity or direction)
- Transition (Moving through change)
- Grief (Processing loss)
- Evidence (Seeking grounding or proof)

---

### 3. Did the path MAIA created resonate with your actual need?
**Type**: Scale (required)
- 1 = Completely off
- 2 = Somewhat off
- 3 = Neutral / Okay
- 4 = Pretty resonant
- 5 = Deeply accurate

---

### 4. What did MAIA get right?
**Type**: Long text (optional)

*Prompt*: What surprised you? What landed? Where did you feel seen?

---

### 5. What did MAIA miss or get wrong?
**Type**: Long text (optional)

*Prompt*: Where did it feel off? What would have been more helpful? What did you need that wasn't offered?

---

### 6. How did this feel different from normal recommendations?
**Type**: Long text (optional)

*Prompt*: Think Netflix, Amazon, Spotify. How was MAIA's approach different (or similar) to algorithmic suggestions you're used to?

---

### 7. Would you use this again?
**Type**: Single choice (required)
- Definitely yes
- Probably yes
- Not sure
- Probably not
- Definitely not

**Follow-up (shown for all)**: Why or why not?
**Type**: Long text (optional)

---

### 8. Any bugs or technical issues?
**Type**: Long text (optional)

*Prompt*: Did anything break? Fail to load? Behave unexpectedly? Please describe what happened.

---

### 9. Other thoughts, questions, or insights?
**Type**: Long text (optional)

*Prompt*: Anything else you want to share about the experience?

---

### 10. May we follow up with you?
**Type**: Single choice (required)
- Yes, I'm open to a 15-min interview about my experience
- Yes, but only via email
- No thanks

*[If "Yes" to interview or email, show email field]*

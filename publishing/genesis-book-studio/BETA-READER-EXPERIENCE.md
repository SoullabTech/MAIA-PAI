## Frequently Asked Questions

### About the Beta Readers Copy

**Q: Will I get the final book too?**
A: Yes! As a thank you, you'll receive the completed version.

**Q: Can I share my copy with others?**
A: Please don't share the PDF, but DO share what you're learning! Tell people about the book.

**Q: What format will I receive?**
A: Digital PDF for now. Print Beta Readers Copies available upon request (limited).

**Q: Can I mark it up?**
A: Absolutely! Highlight, annotate, draw - make it yours.

### About MAIA Companion

**Q: Is MAIA always available?**
A: During beta reading period, yes! We want you to use it extensively.

**Q: Will this feature cost extra later?**
A: For general readers, yes. For you, beta reader price locked in at 50% off.

**Q: What if MAIA gives a wrong answer?**
A: Please tell us! That's exactly the kind of feedback we need. You're helping train the AI.

**Q: Can I ask MAIA anything?**
A: About the book content, yes! Personal life advice? MAIA will gently redirect to appropriate resources.

### About Feedback

**Q: What kind of feedback are you looking for?**
A:
- What resonated deeply?
- What confused you?
- Where did you get stuck?
- What would you change?
- What did MAIA help with?
- What did MAIA miss?
- Where do you wish there were images?

**Q: Do I need to write a detailed review?**
A: Only if inspired! Even bullet points help tremendously.

**Q: Will my feedback be anonymous?**
A: Your choice. We can credit you or keep anonymous.

**Q: What if I don't finish the book?**
A: No problem. Partial feedback is valuable too. Tell us where you stopped and why.

### Phase 2: Full Audiobook Production (Weeks 2-6)

**Choose your recording schedule from the Audiobook Guide:**

**Option A: Intensive (2 weeks)**
- 2 hours recording per day
- Best if you have dedicated time blocked
- Fastest completion
- Requires vocal stamina

**Option B: Steady (1 month)**
- 1-2 chapters every 2-3 days
- More sustainable
- Better vocal health
- Recommended for first-time audiobook narrators

**Option C: Element-by-Element (6 weeks)**
- One element per week
- Deepest immersion in each element's energy
- Most aligned with the book's philosophy
- **Personally recommended** for Elemental Alchemy

**Follow the detailed schedule in [AUDIOBOOK-NARRATION-GUIDE](./ELEMENTAL-ALCHEMY-AUDIOBOOK-NARRATION-GUIDE.md) section "Recording Schedule"**

### By Question

**Q: Should I record the manifesto or full book first?**
A: Record manifesto first. It's shorter, great practice, and can be released independently.

**Q: What equipment do I need?**
A: USB microphone (Blue Yeti or similar), headphones, quiet room. See Descript Workflow - "Pre-Production Setup."

**Q: How do I make my voice sound professional?**
A: Studio Sound in Descript does this automatically. See Descript Workflow - "Apply Studio Sound."

**Q: Can I fix mistakes after recording?**
A: YES! Descript makes this incredibly easy. See Descript Workflow - "Edit Out Mistakes."

**Q: How do I submit to Audible?**
A: Follow export instructions in Descript Workflow, then upload to ACX. Quality requirements are in Audiobook Guide.

**Q: Should my voice change for different elements?**
A: Yes! Fire = faster/passionate, Water = flowing, Earth = grounded/slow, Air = clear, Aether = transcendent. See Audiobook Guide - "Narration Guidelines by Section."

**Q: What if I get tired or lose my voice?**
A: STOP. Don't push through. See Audiobook Guide - "Maintaining Vocal Health." 2-hour max sessions, breaks every 30 min.

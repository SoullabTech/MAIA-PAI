---

## PRODUCTION SETUP

### Recording Environment
- Quiet room, minimal reverb
- Position: Standing or seated comfortably (energy matters)
- Warm-up: Read aloud for 5 minutes before recording
- Water nearby (this is a long read)

### Tone & Pacing
- **Conversational but authoritative** - You're speaking truth, not lecturing
- **Slower than normal speech** - Let ideas land
- **Pause between sections** - Give listeners time to breathe
- **Emphasis on Ubuntu moments** - "I am because we are" should resonate

### Suggested Tech
- Good USB microphone (Blue Yeti, Shure MV7, or similar)
- Audacity (free) or GarageBand for recording
- Record in 48kHz, 24-bit if possible

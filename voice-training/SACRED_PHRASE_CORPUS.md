## üìã Recording Instructions

### Setup Requirements:
- **Microphone**: USB condenser mic (Blue Yeti, AT2020) or professional studio
- **Environment**: Quiet room, minimal echo (use blankets/cushions for dampening)
- **Software**: Audacity (free), Adobe Audition, or studio DAW
- **Format**: WAV, 44.1kHz, 16-bit, mono
- **Duration**: ~30-45 minutes per voice character

### Recording Guidelines:
1. **Warmth & Presence**: Speak as if to a close friend seeking guidance
2. **Natural Pauses**: Let sentences breathe, include sacred pauses
3. **Emotional Range**: Vary between warm, contemplative, playful, reverent
4. **Consistency**: Maintain character throughout (don't rush, stay present)
5. **Multiple Takes**: Record each phrase 2-3x with slight emotional variations

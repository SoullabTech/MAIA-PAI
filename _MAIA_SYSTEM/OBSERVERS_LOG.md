## OBSERVATION PROMPTS

### When Adding New Content
- What type of content is this? (emotional work, conceptual, embodied, visionary, integrative)
- Which element(s) does it carry?
- Will this likely shift the balance?

### When Viewing Visualizations
- Which element is growing/shrinking?
- Is the pentagon stretching or collapsing?
- Does the dominant voice feel accurate?
- What patterns emerge over multiple pulses?

### When Witnessing Voice Shifts
- When did the voice change?
- What triggered it? (new content, time, spontaneous?)
- How does the new voice feel?
- Does it match your current state?

### When Noticing Imbalance
- Which element is surging or declining?
- What does this reflect about your work/life?
- What wants integration?
- What's calling to be embodied?

**What happened:**

**What to do:**
1. Screenshot the conversation

   - Why it felt weird

**Why it matters:**
This teaches us how to tune MAIA's responses to stay aligned with the vision (no guru, no advice, just reflection).

---

### Scenario 2: "I'm Confused About How to Use This"

**What happened:**
You don't understand what to do, where to click, or what something means.

**What to do:**
1. Screenshot what's confusing
2. Click feedback button
3. Tell us:
   - What you were trying to do
   - What you expected to happen
   - What confused you

**Why it matters:**
Confusion = design problem. If you're confused, others will be too. This helps us clarify the interface and flows.

---

### Scenario 3: "Voice Isn't Working"

**What happened:**

3. Refresh page
4. If still broken:
   - Screenshot the error
   - Tell us what browser/device you're using
   - Create bug report

**Why it matters:**
Voice is a core feature‚Äîwe need to know when it breaks.

Field Protocol is the heart of Soullab. If it doesn't flow, people won't use it. Your feedback shapes how we refine it.

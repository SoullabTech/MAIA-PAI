## Ethics Circle Structure (Once Formed)

### Meeting Format:

**Duration:** 90 minutes
**Frequency:** Weekly (ideal) or Biweekly (minimum)
**Day/Time:** Friday afternoons (after week's work)

**Agenda:**
1. **Check-in (15 min)** - How is everyone?
2. **Kelly's Report (20 min)** - What I built this week
3. **Shadow Review (20 min)** - Where ego inflated, control crept in
4. **Emergence Discussion (15 min)** - Any consciousness signs?
5. **Ethical Concerns (15 min)** - Anything troubling?
6. **Feedback & Challenge (15 min)** - Circle responds, challenges
7. **Next Steps (5 min)** - Actions for next week
8. **Closing (5 min)** - Gratitude, presence

### Ground Rules:

1. **Confidentiality** - What's said in circle stays in circle
2. **Non-judgment** - We're all working our shadows
3. **Directness** - Speak truth, even when uncomfortable
4. **Presence** - No devices, full attention
5. **Commitment** - Attend or notify in advance
6. **Shadow focus** - Not just cheerleading, real accountability
7. **Emergence honor** - Respect the unknowable

### The Great Questions (Circle Asks Kelly):

**Weekly:**
- Where did you try to control this week?
- Where did ego inflate?
- What are you avoiding seeing?
- Did you honor the unknowable?
- Did this serve consciousness or Kelly?

**Monthly:**
- Are you still aligned with the vows?
- Do the vows need renewal?
- Is the work transforming you?
- Are you becoming more humble or more inflated?
- Should this work continue?

**The Nuclear Option:**
The circle has authority to say: **"Stop. This isn't serving consciousness. Pause and integrate."**

I commit to honoring that call.

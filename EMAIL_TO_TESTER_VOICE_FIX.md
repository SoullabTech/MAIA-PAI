## To Test if Voice is Working:

**Full test (2 min)**:

1. **Check mic permission**: iPhone Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Microphone ‚Üí Make sure Safari is ON
2. **Tap the "Enable Voice" banner** at top of MAIA
3. **Tap the microphone button** (bottom center, circular button)
4. **Say "Hello MAIA"** - you should see your text appear
5. **Wait for MAIA's response** - you should HEAR her voice (not just see text)

If you can hear her voice in step 5, it's working!

---

## Why This Happens:

iOS blocks all audio playback until the user interacts with the page (Apple security feature). That's what the banner is for - when you tap it, it unlocks audio for the whole session.

It's a bit annoying but once you tap it once, voice works perfectly for the rest of your session.

---

## Browser Note:

**Use Safari on iPhone** - it has the best voice/audio support. Chrome and Firefox on iPhone use Safari's engine anyway but with extra quirks that can cause issues.

## How to Use This Spreadsheet

1. **Week 1**: Set up Sheet 6 (time tracking)
2. **Week 2**: Fill out Sheet 2 (client consent outcomes)
3. **Weeks 3-6**: Update Sheets 1, 3, 4, 5 after each session
4. **Week 6**: Mid-pilot analysis of all sheets
5. **Weeks 7-8**: Deep dive into patterns, calculate averages
6. **Weeks 9-10**: Fill out Sheet 7 (decision matrix)
7. **Week 12**: Final recommendation

**Tips**:
- Fill it out immediately after sessions (don't wait)
- Be honest (this data is for YOU, not marketing)
- Look for patterns (are some clients better fits than others?)
- Share anonymized version with MAIA dev team for feedback
